   The Key Lab was established in July 1995 sponsored by the science & technology commission of shanghai municipality. It is an opening laboratory and important intermediate pilot base for metallic functional materials being among the advanced-level ones in china.


   We, collaborated with Shanghai Steel & Iron Research Institute, have co-built the Key Lab for Metallic Functional Material R&D in Tongji University in 2002.


   As a result, the research base lies in Tongji university. Application & Development ones locate in Shanghai Iron&Steel Research Institute.


  The Key Lab is devoted to provide technical service and cooperate with various research organization.In the past few years, we have accompolished over 10 projects with some university just like Jiaotong University,Tongji University and Shanghai University etc. With the process of co-building, the Key Lab will be challenged to be a higher level.
